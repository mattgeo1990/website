Research Interests

I study stable isotope geochemistry, with a focus on reconstructing paleoenvironments and hydroclimate using oxygen isotopes from fossil teeth and carbonates. My research explores climate dynamics and terrestrial ecosystem evolution during critical intervals in Earth’s history.
